About Samuel Abu

A Relentless Advocate For Justice

At The OV Advisory, we understand that facing criminal charges is one of the most stressful experiences in a person's life. I, Samuel Abu, have dedicated my career to navigating the complex Nigerian legal system to ensure my clients receive the fair defense they deserve.

With an office centrally located in Maryland, Lagos, I am positioned to handle high-profile cases across the state. My approach is simple: thorough investigation, aggressive representation, and an unwavering commitment to the "Not Guilty" verdict.

Whether you are facing white-collar allegations or assault charges, I bring a strategic mindset that has secured a 95% success rate for my clients over the past decade.
