Our History & Vision

The OV Advisory was founded in 2020 with a clear vision to provide innovative, practical, and client-focused legal services to individuals, businesses, and public sector institutions. The firm has grown steadily to distinguish itself in the provision of first-class legal services, offering legal guidance and support of the highest standard to a wide range of local and international clients.

Our clients have come to value our ability to achieve successful outcomes in litigation cases; give expert and pragmatic advice; and provide conscientious and responsive service. We formulate legal strategies that will ensure the continued and effective flow of your daily business operations. Our commitment to excellence, integrity, and responsiveness has earned us a reputation for delivering results and building lasting relationships with our clients.

We have our offices in **Lagos** and **Abuja** and are working towards opening more branches in order to have a wide range of legal services coverage, all in a bid to shoulder the burden of our clients. We work wherever our clients need us. This is how we define ourselves, not by reference to where we have offices. Cross border work isn't just what we do; it is what we excel at.
